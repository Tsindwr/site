### The Magisterium of Empir Draconi _(Pre-1354)_

- **Archmagister Thalvorius the Adamant** - A revered scholar and master of elemental transmutation, Thalvorius was one of the last voices of caution before Vethiskaar's Folly. He sought to stabilize [[The Arras]] but was ultimately silenced by the emperor's decree.
- **Magister Iridessa Vhalar** - A brilliant artificer who specialized in aetherstone technology. Some of her surviving blueprints hint at undiscovered secrets of levitation magic.
- **Magister Rylthorne the Vexed** - Known for his controversial research into the fusion of draconic essence with mortals, Rylthorne's experiments were outlawed, but rumors suggest his work continued in secret enclaves.

---

### The Riftborne Survivors _(Post-1354)_

- **Veylin "Ashcloak" Draevan** - A former sky-noble turned warlord, Veylin commands one of the strongest enclaves within the Docking Rings, leading scavengers in reclaiming lost arcana from the fallen empire.
- **Orvessa the Hollow-Eyed** - A mystic who wandered into the Kagecombs and returned with cryptic knowledge. She claims to hear the whispers of the empire’s lost souls.
- **Jaspar Flintwing** - A courier and wind-skiff pilot who specializes in navigating the Wastes of Draconi, offering passage and illicit trade between scattered enclaves.

---

### The Chain of the Rift _(Cult of the Fallen Empire)_

- **High Seer Kaelith Morryn** - Leader of the Chain, a sect that believes the empire's destruction was divine punishment for their hubris and seeks to bind the shattered world in servitude to a higher order.
- **Inquisitor Zareth Vox** - A ruthless enforcer of the Chain’s doctrine, known for hunting down rogue arcanists and apostates in the ruins.
- **Elder Drenmar Thalvos** - An ex-Magister who repurposed his knowledge of [[The Arras]] to serve the Chain, creating rituals designed to suppress the lingering magical anomalies of the empire.

## Historical Figures

> [!danger]- **Empress Vorrashka the Skyborne** _(r. 512-588)_ 
> One of the few non-draconic rulers in the empire’s history, Vorrashka was a storm genasi whose command of elemental magic earned her a throne among the empire’s elite. Her rule is remembered as a golden age of interplanar diplomacy.

> [!danger]- **Emperor Kael’Zyrrith the Gilded** _(r. 764-829)_ 
> Known for his economic and territorial expansion, Kael’Zyrrith transformed Empir Draconi into a center of elemental commerce. His reign saw the construction of the first floating cities.

> [!danger]- **Magister Ulthara the Everbound** _(r. 899-955)_ 
> A scholar of immense renown, Ulthara uncovered a means of stabilizing floating cities using Aetherstone resonance, but mysteriously disappeared in a diplomatic mission to the elemental planes.

> [!danger]- **High Talon Serevaal the Blackened** _(d. 1021)_ 
> A legendary warleader who waged campaigns against rogue giant enclaves in the southwestern mountains. His tactics in aerial warfare became the foundation of Empir Draconi’s military doctrine.

> [!danger]- **Vethiskaar the Last** _(d. 1354)_ 
> The final emperor of the Arcane Draconic Empire, Vethiskaar attempted to extend his dominion by manipulating [[The Arras]] to control elemental forces. This catastrophic experiment led to the collapse of his empire and the onset of the elemental storms that defined the Tenechron. His name is now synonymous with hubris among arcane scholars.
